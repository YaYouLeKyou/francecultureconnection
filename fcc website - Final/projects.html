PEDAGOGY, EDUCATION TRAINING AND WORKSHOPS LEARNING PLAYFUL

Local
and international

We organize local and international projects to provide opportunities for disadvantaged groups to improve their competences through concerts, workshops, seminars, trainings and events through intercultural meetings. We also organize local events in order to obtain funds to carry out social actions. We are conducting following activities:

• Technology and digital: We offer training about technology, programming, planning models, technology integration models, and digital tools that can be integrated into the learning process, such as Web 2.0 tools, design of multimedia, educational videos, augmented/virtual reality environments, gamification, and as well as training about design of blended learning, flipped learning, educational games, and gamification for youth workers and teachers.

• Music: We provide musical training for citizens including playing instruments, singing lessons, mix and mastering training and video shooting techniques.

• Artistic: We provide workshops regarding theater, puppetry, digital storytelling to help disadvantaged groups actively take part in society as well as improving their artistic skills.

• Environment: We offer trainings and workshops about planting, environmental protection, agriculture, composting, waste management, food waste, recycling, green entrepreneurship.

• Entrepreneurship: We offer trainings regarding digital marketing, social media content creation, entrepreneurship.

• Human rights education: We organize training and workshops for disadvantaged groups like women, LGBTQ+, migrants to inform them about human rights and their specific rights, organizing online and face-to-face campaign, producing digital contents to raise their voices and increase their visibilities and creation of policy recommendation paper.

OUR PROJECTS ERASMUS + KA1 & KA2

KA2: HIDDEN GENIUSES PROJEC 2023-2024

France, Turkey, Greece and Spain gathered around an educational project on IT: Enhancing the digital knowledge, skills, and competences of our organization’s staff. Exchanging good practices in utilizing ICT in youth work. Promoting the inclusion of disadvantaged youth groups.

Theater against hate speach

Racist and intolerant hate speech in public discourse is escalating among the youth in Europe. Therefore, We decided to organize a youth exchange between 8th-17th July 2022 in Strasbourg, France with 31 youngsters from France, Turkey, Spain, Greece and Romania to create awareness among youth about hate speech and encourage them to combat with it through theater as well as starting a new campaign against hate speech by theater methodology.

Voices 4 inclusion

Youth exchange music project with France, Spain, Italie, Germany welcomded by Simaya between 30th may to 7th July 2024 in Villanueva de la Concepción, Andalusia, Spain.

United choir

Youth Exchange with France, Greece, Germany, Turkey, Latvia, Spain about gender awareness raising tthrough choir music to increase general sensitivity, understanding and knowledge of youngsters 17th-27th April, Paralia Greece

(C)hoir for (H)arm (O)ny w(I)thout bo(R)ders

Green Horizons

Training course to support each other in developing local strategies and action plans that teach and involve young people in caring more for our planet. - Learning about important environmental issues and how to live sustainably. - Getting better at planning and running environmental actions. - Networking and forming collaborations for future environmental projects.

Harmonic Voices Against Racism

Racist prejudice and discrimination increased in European countries due to Covid. As ChangeMakers Luxembourg, working with people from different ethnic groups, decided to organise a Youth Exchange to make contribution to this common problem of the Europe with international collaboration to develop a dialogue among society..
