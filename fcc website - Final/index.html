France Culture Connection is a new established NGO aiming to help people, especially disabled and disadvantaged having social, economic, cultural and geographical obstacles, from the all segment of the society to improve their skills, to combat with discrimination, racism, intolerance to enable them to take an active part in the society. We also help them to find suitable education and/or employment both in the these fields through educational, cultural, sportive and social projects at national and international level

The mission of our organization

promote digital transition, green transition and social inclusion through realization of artistic, sportive, environmental and digital projects. We organise symposiums, seminars, conferences, round tables in the field of culture, sport environment, digital transformation as well as music competitions and festivals in the country and abroad. We educate young talented people and organise public events for them. We do charity activities aimed at assisting young people who need financial and other support.

Our Story

ABOUT US

France Culture Connection is a cultural exchange NGO founded in 2020, focus on the field of education, culture, digital, arts and sports. We organize local and international projects to provide opportunities for disadvantaged groups to improve their competences through, workshops, seminars, trainings and events through intercultural meetings.
