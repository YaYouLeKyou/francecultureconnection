Our Story

France Culture Connection is an established intercultural exchange NGO aiming to help people, especially disabled and disadvantaged having social, economic, cultural and geographical obstacles, from the all segment of the society to improve their skills, to combat with discrimination, racism, intolerance to enable them to take an active part in the society. We also help them to find suitable education and/or employment both in the these fields through educational, cultural, sportive and social projects at national and international level  

The mission of our organization is to promote digital transition, green transition and social inclusion through realization of artistic, sportive, environmental and digital projects. We organise symposiums, seminars, conferences, round tables in the field of culture, sport environment, digital transformation as well as music competitions and festivals in the country and abroad. We educate young talented people and organise public events for them. We do charity activities aimed at assisting young people who need financial and other support.

International Arts + Mind Lab

We organize local and international projects to provide opportunities for disadvantaged groups to improve their competences through concerts, workshops, seminars, trainings and events through intercultural meetings. We also organize local events in order to obtain funds to carry out social actions. We are conducting following activities:
• Technology and digital: We offer training about technology, programming, planning models, technology integration models, and digital tools that can be integrated into the learning process, such as Web 2.0 tools, design of multimedia, educational videos, augmented/virtual reality environments, gamification, and as well as training about design of blended learning, flipped learning, educational games, and gamification for youth workers and teachers.
• Music: We provide musical training for citizens including playing instruments, singing lessons, mix and mastering training and video shooting techniques.
• Artistic: We provide workshops regarding theater, puppetry, digital storytelling to help disadvantaged groups actively take part in society as well as improving their artistic skills.
• Environment: We offer trainings and workshops about planting, environmental protection, agriculture, composting, waste management, food waste, recycling, green entrepreneurship.
• Entrepreneurship: We offer trainings regarding digital marketing, social media content creation, entrepreneurship.
• Human rights education: We organize training and workshops for disadvantaged groups like women, LGBTQ+, migrants to inform them about human rights and their specific rights, organizing online and face-to-face campaign, producing digital contents to raise their voices and increase their visibilities and creation of policy recommendation paper.
